Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does HalalScan determine if a product is halal?
    The app analyzes a product's ingredient list using a combination of AI analysis and a multi-language keyword database covering known haram and suspicious ingredients.
  • What do the verdict labels mean?
    Halal — no haram ingredients detected. Not Halal — one or more haram ingredients found. Suspicious — contains ingredients that may be haram depending on source or processing.
  • The app didn't find my product. What can I do?
    Make sure the barcode is well-lit and in focus. If the product still isn't found, it may not yet be in the database — you can report it via the in-app feedback option or by emailing support.
  • Is my scan history stored anywhere?
    Scan history is stored locally on your device. Anonymous product lookups are cached on the server to improve performance for all users; no personal data is linked to them.
  • How do I delete my local scan history?
    Open the app, go to History, and use the clear/delete option in the menu.
