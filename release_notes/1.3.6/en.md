**What's new in 1.3.6**

- **Community display name** — After sign-in, confirm or choose the name shown in discussions and comments. Change it anytime from your profile menu.
- **Profile role** — Your account menu now shows your role (e.g. Member, Moderator, Scholar, Admin).
- **Smoother Google sign-in** — On iPhone and Android, sign in with the native Google account picker when possible, with a browser fallback if needed. Fixes an issue that could block sign-in on iOS.
- **More reliable scan history on Android** — Improved local database setup so your scan history saves more reliably.
